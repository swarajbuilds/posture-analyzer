📋 About Our Posture AI Project ASEP II — Group 7 Project

AI-Powered Ergonomic Posture Monitor & Eye Strain Guard

We built this system to help students and professionals protect their spine and eyes while working on computers. Sitting at a desk for long hours can cause neck pain, shoulder tension, and eye strain.

Using a normal webcam, our system monitors your posture completely in the browser (processed entirely on your own computer for absolute privacy!). It calculates your alignment, counts your eye blinks, and helps you stay healthy.

Everything runs smoothly and securely in real-time, giving you helpful visual charts and gauges to see how your posture changes throughout your session.

✨ Key Features We Built:

  • 📐
    Real-Time Posture Tracking Instantly measures shoulder slant, head tilt, and neck alignment to tell you if you are slanting or leaning.
  • 👁️
    Eye Strain & Blink Monitoring Automatically tracks your blink count to protect you from screen dryness and fatigue.
  • 🔔
    Stretch Breaks & Reminders Sends you quick alerts and friendly stretch exercises when you sit in a bad posture for too long.
